CoCo Qiu

Founder

Hi, I'm CoCo! I am a current senior at Madison County High School, based in a small town in Georgia. I have played for Gwinnett Soccer Association (GSA) ECNL for the past 3 years. I recently went through the recruitment process and wanted to help provide insights and awareness for the aspiring Asian American athletic community that I once wished I had. Through my own experiences, it has created my motivations for expanding equitable access to opportunity and standardizing the playing field for people who come from less resourceful areas/backgrounds. Outside of soccer, I spend a lot of time frolicking outdoors and love a good coffee chat!

Olivia Lee

Mentor / Panel Speaker

Hi, I'm Olivia! I'm a student-athlete at the University of Pittsburgh, where I balance a double major in Finance and Marketing with my role as a leader on the women's soccer team. Originally from Scarsdale, New York, I grew up playing for New York Soccer Club in the Girls' Academy League before committing to Pitt as a high school junior. After earning significant playing time as a freshman and sophomore, I was honored to be named Team Captain in my junior year. Off the field, I'm a huge dog lover and I love to go to the beach!
