CoCo Qiu

Founder

Hi, I'm CoCo! I am a current senior at Madison County High School, based in a small town in Georgia. I have played for Gwinnett Soccer Association (GSA) ECNL, Concorde Fire ECNL, and ODP over the past 4 years. During this time, I have helped lead my team to ECNL Nationals for 3 consecutive years. I recently went through the recruitment process and wanted to help provide insights and awareness for the aspiring Asian American athletic community that I once wished I had. Through my own experiences, it has created my motivations for expanding equitable access to opportunity and standardizing the playing field for people who come from less resourceful areas/backgrounds. Outside of soccer, I spend a lot of time frolicking outdoors and love a good coffee chat!

Skyla Yu

Co-Founder

Hi, I'm Skyla! I'm currently a senior at Liberty High School in Washington and I play for Pac Northwest ECNL. I've committed to Colorado School of Mines, where I'll continue playing soccer while studying engineering. I went through a highly competitive recruitment process, and that experience inspires me to help other Asian athletes navigate their journey! Outside of soccer, I love traveling, spending time with friends, and eating yummy food!

Arianna Chin

Co-Founder

Hi, I'm Arianna! I am a current senior at Sacred Heart Greenwich in Connecticut, and am committed to MIT for soccer. I currently play for Connecticut Football Club (CFC) ECNL. While going through my own recruitment process, I realized that there's not a lot of Asian American representation in sports. Thus, I am motivated to help the younger generation gain more insight about recruitment and provide them with opportunities I never had. Outside of soccer, I enjoy listening to music, reading, and hanging out with my friends!

Olivia Lee

Mentor / Panel Speaker

Hi, I'm Olivia! I'm a student-athlete at the University of Pittsburgh, where I balance a double major in Finance and Marketing with my role as a leader on the women's soccer team. Originally from Scarsdale, New York, I grew up playing for New York Soccer Club in the Girls' Academy League before committing to Pitt as a high school junior. After earning significant playing time as a freshman and sophomore, I was honored to be named Team Captain in my junior year. Off the field, I'm a huge dog lover and I love to go to the beach!

Viet-Thy Tran

Mentor / Panel Speaker

Hi! My name is Viet-Thy Tran, but I go by Thy. I'm a junior midfielder on the soccer team at the University of California, Davis, where I'm also majoring in computer science. I was born and raised in Santa Clara, California, and grew up playing soccer for Bay Area Surf and futsal for San Jose Legends. While preparing for my final season at UC Davis, I'm proud to also represent the United States as a member of the U.S. Futsal National Team. Off the field, I enjoy spending time in nature and exploring new food spots whenever I get the chance.
